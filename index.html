SHE DIDN'T FIT THE MOLD.
SHE BROKE IT.

EVA MARIE started as a refusal. A refusal to choose between performance and style. Between the gym and the going-out scene. Between power and femininity.

We design activewear and clubwear for women who don't make those choices — because they never had to. Every set, every top, every bottom in this collection is built for the woman who shows up for herself and shows out for everyone watching.

Crafted in compression knits, washed cottons, and ribbed fabrics with our signature EM monogram — every piece is designed to move, to hold, and to make a statement the moment you put it on.

EVA MARIE is for all of her. The 6am workout her. The Saturday night her. The Tuesday afternoon running errands her. Wear it. Own it. All of it.
